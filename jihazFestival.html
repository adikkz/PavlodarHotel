Our celebration of life at home made its return, this time focusing on people’s real creativity and inventiveness at home. The Jihaz Festival took place online, as well as in Jihaz stores around the world, from August 26 to September 10. The good news is that it's still available to explore. Tune in and discover something new!

A round-the-world tour

Over the course of the festival, some of the world's most-loved Jihaz stores played host for a day, sharing non-stop entertainment and inspiration from nine cities all around the globe. If the Jihaz Festival came to your city, we hope you were able to join in on the fun!
